What are your favorite books? Prunella enjoys these.

King Midas: A Golden Tale
by Omar Rayyan
A greedy king wishes that everything he touches would turn to gold. Find out what happens when his wish comes true.
Pegasus
by Marianna Mayer
Pegasus, the winged horse, is my favorite mythological beast. You'll love the pictures and the story.
The Race of the Golden Apples
by Claire Martin
The princess Atalanta is the swiftest runner in the kingdom. She will only agree to marry a man who can outrun her.
Wings
by Jane Yolen
This is the story of Daedalus and his son Icarus who build wings of wax and feathers and learn to fly.
