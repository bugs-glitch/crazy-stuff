What are your favorite books? Francine enjoys these.

Amber Brown is Not a Crayon!
by Paula Danziner
I can really relate to this story about Amber and her best friend, Justin.
The Ballad of the Pirate Queens
by Jane Yolen
Go back in time to meet Anne Bonney and Mary Reade, who were really pirates in the 1700s.
Meet Danitra Brown
by Nikki Grimes
This is a great collection of poems about Danitra, the "most splendiferous girl in town" and her best friend, Zuri.
Zora Hurston and the Chinaberry Tree
by William Miller
Did you ever hear of the famous author Zora Neale Hurston? This is the story of how she came to be a writer.
